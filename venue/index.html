Venue

Fortunately, we found an incredible venue in Cologne, accommodating about 200 humans, and allowing all sorts of activities and spontaneous RailsCamp craziness to happen. If you want, you can even set up tent in the the neighboring skate hall, so you don't have to leave during the whole weekend. And how about a climb up the walls during the day?

And so you get there:

Car, Spaceship & Hovercraft

Here are your coordinates:
Abenteuerhallen Kalk
Christina-Sünner-Straße 8
51103 Köln
(50.9352007 7.00926661)

Additional information is available on the website of the Abenteuerhallen.

Public Transport

Subway/Underground
Take "Stadtbahn" #1 or #9 to station Kapelle Kalk
Walk along Wiersbergstr. till you get to an old entrance gate: Go straight ahead and you see the Abenteuerhalle on the right.

Apps
Deutsche Bahn provides traveling information for most smartphones via the DB Navigator App. (Tickets also available via HandyTicket App)
